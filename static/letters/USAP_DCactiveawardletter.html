Welcome to the United States Antarctic Program Data Center. USAP-DC supports NSF-funded researchers in fulfilling your data management obligations* and provides a central record of NSF-funded Antarctic projects and data. Your NSF OPP award ***NUMBER - TITLE*** can now be registered at www.usap-dc.org.

Complete the webform at https://www.usap-dc.org/submit/project to be guided through the necessary steps to register your project and create a project page. For Collaborative Research projects, there should be only one project page - we recommend that the PI of the lead institution sets up the project page and can link the other awards during registration. Confirm with your program manager if you think that your award will not require a project page (e.g., workshop proposals).

Throughout the duration of the project, named project personnel can update the project page with new datasets and publications. As datasets become available, they should be linked to the project either through submission for archiving at the USAP-DC or as links to external archives. We will use the information you provide to create an entry for your project in the Antarctic Master Database. Project pages can also be created for past awards.

If you have any questions please reach out to us at info@usap-dc.org or see the help pages for project registration, the data archive and the FAQ.

Best Regards,
