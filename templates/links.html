4. Data Resources

U.S. Based

  • Antarctic Ice Velocity Data, NSIDC – Compilation of recent ice velocity data of the Antarctic ice sheet.
  • Antarctic Meteorology Research Center & Antarctic Weather Stations Project, University of Wisconsin – Antarctic meteorological data for USAP, iceberg imagery, real-time meteorological data.
  • Biological and Chemical Oceanography Data Management Office (BCO-DMO) – Repository for biological, chemical, physical oceanographic, and marine ecosystem data.
  • EarthChem – Data management for Solid Earth Geochemistry.
  • Geographical Names Information System, USGS – Search for names of geographic features in Antarctica.
  • ICE-D Antarctica - informal cosmogenic-nuclide exposure-age database.
  • Incorporated Research Institutions for Seismology (IRIS) – Archive for seismographic data including from the Global Seismographic Network and PASSCAL programs.
  • Marine Geoscience Data System (MGDS), part of IEDA - Repository for geophysical data including the Global Multi-Resolution Topography (GMRT) synthesis.
  • NOAA Global Monitoring Division, South Pole Observatory.
  • Long Term Ecological Research Network (LTERnet), McMurdo Dry Valleys.
  • OSU Marine and Geology Repository, Oregon State University – Repository for core and dredge samples from polar regions.
  • Polar Geospatial Center (PGS) - geospatial support, mapping, and GIS/remote sensing solutions.
  • Reference Elevation Model of Antarctica (REMA) - high resolution, time-stamped Digital Surface Model (DSM) of Antarctica.
  • Rolling Deck to Repository - (R2R), Master catalog of U.S. research cruises including USAP vessels.
  • USGS U.S. Antarctic Resource Center (USARC) – Collection of Antarctic maps, charts, satellite images, and photographs, provided by USGS.
  • University NAVSTAR Consortium (UNAVCO) Polar Programs – GPS technology support to USAP and geodetic data archive.
  • U.S. GLOBEC Data System – Biological, physical and chemical data collected during GLOBEC programs.
  • Antarctic Photo Library, USAP

International

  • British Antarctic Survey – Polar Data Centre
  • Alfred-Wegener-Institut (AWI) Bathymetry & Geodesy Group
  • Australian Antarctic Division – Data management and spatial data services to Australia's Antarctic program.
  • Antarctic Seismic Data Library System for Cooperative Research (SDLS) – International collaborative effort to archive seismic reflection data from around Antarctica.

6. List Server

The U.S. SCAR Office maintains the ANtarctic Science WEb Resource (ANSWER), a list server intended to disseminate information about Antarctica, communicate opportunities, solicit input on important issues, and to promote Antarctic polar science. To subscribe, send email to: answer-subscribe@usscar.org. A digest of past news is available online.
